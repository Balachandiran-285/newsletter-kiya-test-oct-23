PRODUCT UPDATES

GST Automation Module

Kiya.ai’s GST Automation Module manages the GST, invoicing & accounting as per the new tax regime. The solution is built on modular principles and has the flexibility to be tailored to region-specific needs of the organisation. It provides substantial flexibility in terms of defining hierarchy and parameters can be set to accommodate the business flow of the institution.

Finnet 2.0

Finnet 2.0, developed by Kiya.ai, provides standardised reports that enable financial institutions to meet regulatory requirements efficiently. The standardised format ensures consistency and simplifies reporting, saves time and resources for financial institutions.

FATCA

Kiya.ai, has recently led to the development of tailored FATCA reports for India, China, Belgium, Samoa, and Ghana. By providing comprehensive and accurate reports, Kiya.ai supports financial institutions in meeting their reporting obligations and staying compliant with the respective tax authorities' guidelines.

AML in Exchange House

Kiya.ai’s Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Solution for exchange houses proactively helps financial institutions to identify and report suspicious activities, minimising the risk of financial crimes. The AML solution fortifies the compliance procedures, offering configurable risk parameters and rules that align with our specific requirements.
